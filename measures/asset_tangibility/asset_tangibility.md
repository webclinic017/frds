# Asset Tangibility

## Definition

Property, Plant and Equipment (Net) scaled by Assets (Total).

$$
\text{Asset Tangibility}_{i,t}=\frac{PPENT_{i,t}}{AT_{i,t}}
$$

where $PPENT$ and $AT$ are from Compustat Fundamentals Annual `WRDS.COMP.FUNDA`.
