    Capital Expenditure¶

    Definition¶

    The capital expenditures scaled by total assets.

    \text{Capital Expenditure}_{i,t} = \frac{CAPX_{i,t}}{AT_{i,t}}

    where CAPX and AT are from Compustat Fundamentals Annual WRDS.COMP.FUNDA.
